## Style Prompt
Clarity uses the opening Figma frame as a quiet illustrated game world: warm hazy sun, muted green-gray mountains, soft beige sky, dark evergreen silhouettes, and a pale campus tower. The motion should feel like a calm parallax game intro, not a flashy trailer.

## Colors
- Sky haze: #8f978d
- Sun glow: #ffe9a8
- Warm tower: #d8b892
- Mountain midtone: #6f8980
- Evergreen dark: #172f31
- Overlay text: rgba(255,255,255,.72)

## Typography
- Use serif text that matches the portfolio mood and approximates the Sanchez caption softness from Figma.
- Keep UI copy small, subdued, and cinematic.

## What NOT to Do
- Do not use neon colors or hard sci-fi styling.
- Do not use photo assets for the game preview.
- Do not add large marketing text over the animation.
- Do not rely on temporary Figma asset URLs.
