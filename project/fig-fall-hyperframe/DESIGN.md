# Fig Fall HyperFrame Design

## Style Prompt
Use the existing portfolio language: stark monochrome, quiet literary texture, italic manuscript text, and a single engraved fig illustration as the moving subject. The animation should feel like a printed page being disturbed by gravity, not like a generic motion graphic.

## Colors
- `#0a0a0a` background
- `#f0f0f0` primary manuscript ink
- `rgba(240,240,240,0.42)` secondary manuscript ink
- `rgba(0,0,0,0.35)` fig shadow

## Typography
- Lora, Georgia, serif for quote text
- VT323, monospace for small attribution text

## What NOT To Do
- Do not use bright accent colors.
- Do not add gradient orb decoration.
- Do not use modern UI cards or panels.
- Do not make the fig look glossy or synthetic.
